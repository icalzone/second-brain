A growing collection of guides and research for planning U.S. national park trips — road trips, RV travel, photography, seasonal timing, and specific park itineraries.

## Road Trips

### Utah's Mighty Five
Utah's five iconic parks can be toured as a loop in **7–10 days**: Zion → Bryce Canyon → Capitol Reef → Canyonlands → Arches. **Highway 12 Scenic Byway** connects key segments through striking desert terrain. Start and end near Zion.

Three nearby state parks are recommended detours if time allows. The loop works as a circuit returning to Zion.

## RV Travel

Ten RV-friendly parks to prioritize. Most require **advance reservations** via [recreation.gov](https://www.recreation.gov/) or park lodge systems.

**Yellowstone** RV campgrounds: Bridge Bay, Canyon, Fishing Bridge, Grant Village, Madison (all reservable). **Mammoth Campground** is first-come, first-served Oct 15–Apr 1.
