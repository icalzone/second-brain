Best Practices for Weather Prompts
Be Specific on Tone: Ask for "friendly," "professional," "concise," or "dramatic" tones.
Define Length: Limit the response to a word count or number of sentences (e.g., "< 160 characters").
Include Data Points: Ensure raw data includes temperature (high/low), precipitation probability, wind speed, and humidity.
Format Constraints: Ask for specific output formats like bullet points, a short paragraph, or JSON.
